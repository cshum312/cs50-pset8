Travel

Over the past few years, I've travelled to a few countries around the world.

Here are some of my thoughts.

Asia

This is the continent of my origin. I've been to and lived in many places here, including China, Japan, Korea, and various places in South East Asia.

Food here is wonderful, with lots of spices. Unfortunately, I can't eat spicy food without crying so my options are limited... But it's incredibly culturally diverse.

Africa

I've never had the opportunity to travel here yet. Though I really want to see the wildlife in the safaris.

North America

I haven't explored it as much as I'd like to. Though I've been to a few states and provinces so far. There's still much to discover.

One thing to note, the portion sizes for the food is huge...

South America

I haven't been here either. Though I heard Columbia is a wonderful place. I'd like to visit someday.

Europe

I had the opportunity to travel around Western and Central Europe. I still have a lot to discover, especially for Eastern Europe.

Food here is wonderful and the ease of travelling between different countries is eye-opening.

Oceania

I went to Australia and New Zealand. Both are wonderful places to visit animals at various times of the year.

Please be wary of the spiders!

Antartica

As much as I enjoy cold weather, I haven't been up to 'The Ice' yet.

Though I'd love to go and watch the penguins at least once in my lifetime!
